Fried Rice ala Mommy

About the Dish

This dish from Kusina la Sofia's exclusive menu: Mommy's Specials.
This was inspired by how my wife loves my fried rice. So, I made a dish
out of the classic Asian Fried Rice with some added flare and flavor.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Green Onions
  • Leftover Rice (preferably refrigerated)
  • Eggs
  • Meat (personal preference, I prefer Chorizo and Bacon)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder

Instructions

  1. Cook the meat until golden brown and crispy
  2. Put in the Chorizo and cook til aromatic
  3. Add the Onions and the Garlic and saute til aromatic
  4. Break down the Rice since its been refrigerated, so grind them down with your hands until granules are visible.
  5. Add the leftover rice to the pan and saute for a couple of minutes
  6. Add Oyster Sauce and then Soy Sauce and mix well
  7. Make space at the center of the pan and add eggs. Scramble the eggs at the center then mix thoroughly with the rice.
  8. Cut the Green onions and add as Toppings.
  9. Add Black pepper and Garlic powder to taste and mix well
  10. Serve while hot
