Baby Sisig (Pork Sisig

About the dish

This dish is again from Kusina la Sofia's exclusives, the Pork Sisig.
During our college days when we used to go out on dates, we barely
had any money for anything expensive. So what we usually did was
I would cook us some food and we would just sit at the bay area and
have our dates there. She always loved my cooking and that's why
this dish is on the list for Top 3.

Ingredients

  • Pork (preferably the face and ears)
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Green Onions
  • Chili
  • Ginger
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Vinegar (Optional)
  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)
  • Kalamansi Juice
  • Bay Leaves

Instructions

  1. Grill the meat (smoking has a very aromatic effect).
  2. Once cooked, cut the meet in small cubes.
  3. after prepping the spices, saute the garlic and ginger first until it smells good.
  4. Once its ready, add the meat and season with pepper (cook on high for only about 3 mins, mix well).
  5. Add oyster sauce and soy sauce.
  6. Add lime or kalamansi Juice. Vinegar is optional. Then add the bay leaves with the chili along with it.
  7. Cook on high while stirring constantly for about 5 minutes or until cooked to preference
  8. Turn off the heat and add the egg. Mix thoroughly.
  9. Serve on a sizzling plate. Add mayonnaise and pepper to taste.
